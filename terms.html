2. Description of Service

Lumen Kids is an educational video platform designed for children ages 0 to 12 under parental supervision. The Service includes:

  • AI-Curated Video Library: We source educational videos from YouTube and use artificial intelligence (specifically Google Gemini) to vet and score them based on educational quality, age-appropriateness, pacing, visual stimulation, and safety standards.
  • Interactive Quizzes: After watching videos, children can complete multiple-choice or voice-based quizzes to reinforce learning.
  • Parental Controls: Parents can set daily screen time limits, manage child profiles, monitor activity history, and receive insights into learning progress through a dedicated dashboard.
  • Parental Dashboard: Parents have access to a dashboard showing watched videos, quiz scores, and personalized talking points generated by AI to facilitate parent-child conversations about the content.
  • Subscription Plans: Two subscription tiers (Essential and Family) provide unlimited access to the video library and features.

The Service is intended to complement, not replace, formal education or professional educational guidance. Content recommendations are AI-generated and may not be suitable for every child or family.
